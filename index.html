How it works

Paus is an online workout program for expecting and postpartum mothers. We offer on-demand workouts for you right in your living room or anywhere you would like!

Here you will find a variety of exercises such as yoga, pilates, mild callisthenics and stretching. We also have our popular meditation guidances, as well as tips for just relaxing and connecting with your baby.

In the Premium plan you can join our interactive live stream sessions and our guest lectures to prepare you mentally and phisically for delivering your baby. Some of these sessions you have the option to join with your partner or whoever will be there to support you during the birth!

Our program divides the exercises into convenient categories, so you can rest assureed that they are appropriate for your trimester. However, we recommend you listen to your doctor's advice if you have a high risk pregnancy or other health complications. After the baby comes, make sure to check out our postpartum recovery videos! We recommend you wait at least 6 weeks before starting to exercise again and with your doctor's approval.

  1. Sign up for our workout program, by selecting the type of plan that best suits your needs.
  2. After entering your billing information, you will then receive a confirmation e-mail prompting you to creata an account.
  3. Click on the link you received to create an account.
  4. Check your e-mail and click on the link to verify your account.
  5. Enter your account details into the required fields of the login section on the top right of this website in order to access the program. Another option is to download our dedicated mobile app to follow the exercises on the go.
  6. That's it! Welcome! You now have access to hundreds of safe exercises for each stage of your pregnancy.

Enjoy taking time to wind down, increase your fitness level and connect with your baby! We are looking forward to hanging out with you!

About Us

Paus is an online workout program intended for expecting and new mothers who wish to have an active nd healthy pregnancy and postpartum recovery of the body and mind. This website is targeted towards women who are looking for safe at-home prenatal and postpartum workouts.

The name Paus comes from Swedish and it has several alternative translations and interpretations that relate to pregnancy and motherhood. It can mean a "waiting period", which every pregnancy is in its essence. It can be to "take a break" and have some time for oneself to focus on their own wellbeing, which is essential for busy mothers. It also relates to "pausing" when it is important to take it slow and listen to your body in times of stress or during postpartum recovery.
